Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This website, HáCZech (www.hackczech.com), respects your privacy. This page explains how I collect, use, and protect your information in compliance with GDPR and Google’s 2026 requirements.

1. Google Analytics & Consent Mode

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how students use our Czech lessons. To protect your privacy in the EEA, we have implemented Google Consent Mode v2.

  • Default State: When you arrive, all advertising and personalization cookies are denied by default.
  • If you "Accept": We use cookies to distinguish users and provide detailed analytics.
  • If you "Reject": We only send anonymous "pings" to Google. These do not store personal data but allow us to count basic website visits.

2. Information I Collect

Newsletter: If you subscribe to the newsletter, I collect your name (if provided) and email address. This is used solely to send you language learning updates.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous data such as browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site to improve our content.

3. Cookies

This site uses cookies to improve your experience. You can manage your consent through the banner that appears on your first visit. You can also withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies. For more details, see Google’s Cookie Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services:

  • Google Analytics: For traffic analysis (IP addresses are anonymized/discarded by Google in the EU).
  • MailerLite: To manage email subscriptions. You can read their policy here.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. You can unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in the footer or by contacting me directly.
