Master the Czech Language with HáCZech

Whether you are starting at A1 level or progressing toward B2, HáCZech offers a robust suite of tools designed to make the Czech language accessible. My Czech Grammar Engine features a database of over 40,000 nouns and verbs, allowing you to verify declensions and conjugations instantly with native audio support.

Vocabulary Lists & Spaced Repetition

Move beyond simple memorization. Use my Spaced Repetition Study Tool (similar to ANKI) to retain words efficiently with picture and audio support. For quick practice, explore my Vocabulary Lists or use Plain Flashcards for rapid review.

Interactive Games & Proficiency Tests

Practice vocabulary in the Games Section through five levels of difficulty: from matching games and multiple-choice quizzes to strict spelling and listening exercises. To track your progress, take one of my Czech Proficiency Tests. Materials are fully supported in English, German, and Spanish.

Gamified Learning

Test your knowledge of the 7 Czech cases in my immersive Czech Escape Game. Save the princess by solving grammar puzzles! It is a fun, visual way to master one of the hardest parts of the language.
