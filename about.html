About HackCzech

HackCzech is an educational website focused on helping learners understand and use the Czech language in a practical, engaging way.

The site offers learning materials, interactive tools, and simple games designed to make Czech grammar, vocabulary, and pronunciation easier to understand.

HackCzech is created and maintained by Lukas, a Czech language teacher with experience helping students of different levels build confidence in Czech.

This project exists to make learning Czech more accessible, practical, and enjoyable.
