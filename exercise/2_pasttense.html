Active Recall Practice: Czech Verbs

This practice session focuses on active production. Instead of just reading verbs, you must mentally generate the past tense form for activities like plavat (to swim) or odpočívat (to rest).

How to Practice Verbs:

Visualize the action while saying the word out loud. For example, when you see Včera jsem vařil, imagine yourself in the kitchen. This builds stronger mental hooks.

Grammar Tips:

Notice the pattern of 'l' endings for past tense. Pay close attention to the auxiliary verb 'jsem' which is essential for first-person past tense in Czech.

Optimization for Your Session:

  • ✔ Step 1: Cycle through cards using Next/Prev.
  • ✔ Step 2: Focus on spelling and diacritics (like in středu or pondělí).
  • ✔ Step 3: Proceed to the 📝 Test once you achieve 100% manual recall.
