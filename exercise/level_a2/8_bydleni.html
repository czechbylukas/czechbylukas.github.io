Training Guide: Mastering Your Domain

Active Recall: Rooms and Repairs

At the A2 level, you need to go beyond naming rooms. You must be able to describe the condition of your home and operate basic spotřebiče (appliances).

Functional Verbs:

Learn the opposites: zapnout (turn on) vs. vypnout (turn off), and the specific light verbs rozsvítit vs. zhasnout.

Renting Logistics:

Understand the relationship between the majitel and the nájem (rent). Is the flat zařízený (furnished) or not?

Optimization Strategy:

  • ✔ Step 1: Visualize your own home. Name every místnost and piece of nábytek as you walk through.
  • ✔ Step 2: Practice the rozbitý (broken) vs. fungovat (to work) distinction for appliances.
  • ✔ Step 3: Once you can explain a basic repair need to a soused, move to the 📝 Test.
