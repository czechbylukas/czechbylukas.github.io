Training Guide: Identity and Relationships

Active Recall: Official Forms & Social Description

This unit bridges the gap between formal documentation and informal storytelling. You will learn to navigate a CV (Životopis) and describe your Family (Rodina).

Bureaucracy & CVs:

In the Czech Republic, the Rodné číslo (birth number) is a unique ID used on all official forms. Pay attention to the difference between křestní jméno (first name) and příjmení (surname).

Physical & Mental Traits:

When describing people, we use vypadat jako (to look like). Practice the difference between mít na sobě (to be wearing right now) and nosit (to wear habitually).

Optimization Strategy:

  • ✔ Step 1: Focus on the "Official" block: stát, adresa, PSČ, podpis.
  • ✔ Step 2: Group family members by gender (e.g., strýc/teta, bratranec/sestřenice).
  • ✔ Step 3: Use the 📝 Test once you can describe both your passport and your best friend.
