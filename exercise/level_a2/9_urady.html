Training Guide: Navigating the System

Active Recall: Applications and Safety

At the A2 level, you transition from simple nouns to "action" phrases required at the úřad. This includes managing your pobyt (residence) and knowing how to ask for pomoc (help).

The Bureaucratic Flow:

You vyplníte (fill in) a formulář, then you podáte (submit) a žádost (application) to an úředník.

Emergencies:

In a krize, you must distinguish between hasiči (firefighters), záchranka (ambulance), and policie. Memorize tísňová linka.

Optimization Strategy:

  • ✔ Step 1: Focus on residence terms: povolení k pobytu, dlouhodobý, trvalý.
  • ✔ Step 2: Master the "submission" verbs: požádat, podat, ohlásit.
  • ✔ Step 3: Once you can explain a ztráta (loss) or krádež (theft), move to the 📝 Test.
