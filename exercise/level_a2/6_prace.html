Training Guide: Career and Labor in Czechia

Active Recall: Professional Identity and Paychecks

Navigating the professional world in Czech requires understanding both job titles and the logistics of employment. This session focuses on the vocabulary you'll find in a životopis (CV) or a pracovní smlouva (contract).

Masculine vs. Feminine Jobs:

Nearly all Czech job titles change based on gender. For example, učitel (male teacher) becomes učitelka (female teacher). Pay close attention to these suffixes.

Salary Logistics:

Crucial for expats: Know the difference between hrubý plat (gross salary) and čistý plat (net salary). You "earn" money using vydělávat.

Optimization Strategy:

  • ✔ Step 1: Categorize jobs by workplace (e.g., nemocnice → lékař, škola → učitel).
  • ✔ Step 2: Master the verb pracovat jako (to work as) followed by the Nominative case.
  • ✔ Step 3: Once you can identify your zaměstnavatel (employer) and your mzda (wage), move to the 📝 Test.
