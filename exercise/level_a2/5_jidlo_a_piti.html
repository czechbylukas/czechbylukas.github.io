Training Guide: Survival in a Czech Restaurant

Active Recall: Ordering and Traditional Meals

Czech dining culture has its own specific set of phrases and iconic dishes. This practice session helps you transition from basic ingredients to full restaurant interactions.

The Ordering Flow:

Learn to recognize the waiter's questions: Máte vybráno? (Ready to order?) and Co si dáte? (What will you have?). Respond confidently with Dám si...

Food Preferences:

Crucial for A2: Learn to state your dietary needs. Mám alergii na... (I have an allergy to...) or bezmasé jídlo (meatless dish).

Optimization Strategy:

  • ✔ Step 1: Memorize the common pre-meal and during-meal phrases (Dobrou chuť, Na zdraví).
  • ✔ Step 2: Familiarize yourself with the "holy trinity" of Czech cooking: vepřo-knedlo-zelo.
  • ✔ Step 3: Once you can ask for the účet (bill) without hesitation, move to the 📝 Test.
