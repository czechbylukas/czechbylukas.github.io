Practice Kontakt s majoritní společností a zařazení do společenského a politického života

Training Guide: Becoming Part of the Community

Active Recall: Social Networks and State Structures

Integrating into the majoritní společnost (majority society) involves two layers: the personal (friends and neighbors) and the formal (laws and government).

Social Circles:

Learn to distinguish between známý (acquaintance), kamarád (friend), and přítel (close friend or partner). Practice verbs like zvát (invite) and scházet se (meet up).

The Czech State:

Understand the structure of the vláda (government) and the two chambers of parlament: the Senát and the Poslanecká sněmovna.

Optimization Strategy:

  • ✔ Step 1: Start with language skills (mluvit, rozumět, překládat).
  • ✔ Step 2: Memorize the administrative divisions: stát, kraj, město, vesnice.
  • ✔ Step 3: Once you can identify your občanství (citizenship) and the role of volby (elections), move to the 📝 Test.
