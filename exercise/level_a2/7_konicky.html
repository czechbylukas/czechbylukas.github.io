Training Guide: Expressing Interests

Active Recall: What Do You Enjoy?

Talking about hobbies in Czech requires specific verb constructions. This session focuses on the difference between general activities and specialized interests.

The "Interest" Verbs:

Learn to use zajímat se o (to be interested in) + Accusative. Also, master the verb bavit: Baví mě sport (Sport entertains/interests me).

Instruments vs. Sports:

In Czech, we use hrát na + Accusative for instruments (hrát na housle), but usually just hrát + Accusative for games (hrát šachy).

Optimization Strategy:

  • ✔ Step 1: Differentiate between dovolená (work vacation) and prázdniny (school holidays).
  • ✔ Step 2: Group verbs of motion: chodit na procházky (walking) vs. jezdit na kole (cycling).
  • ✔ Step 3: Once you can describe your koníček, move to the 📝 Test.
