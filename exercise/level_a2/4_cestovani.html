Training Guide: Navigating the Journey

Active Recall: Directions and Logistics

Traveling in Czechia involves more than just nouns. You need to understand where (kam vs. kde) and how (the Instrumental case).

Means of Transport:

In Czech, we use the 7th case (Instrumental) for transport. Notice how most end in -em: vlakem, autem, autobusem. Except for "on foot," which is pěšky.

Where To? (Kam?):

Distinguish between Kde (position: Kde to je?) and Kam (direction: Kam jedeš?). This is a classic A2 hurdle!

Optimization Strategy:

  • ✔ Step 1: Focus on the "Direction" block: rovně, doleva, doprava.
  • ✔ Step 2: Master the ticket types: jednosměrná (one way) vs. zpáteční (round trip).
  • ✔ Step 3: Once you can ask for a train to Brno, move to the 📝 Test.
