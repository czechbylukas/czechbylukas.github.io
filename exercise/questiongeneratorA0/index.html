Training Guide: Mastering Czech Conversation

Active Speaking: From Grammar to Reality

At the A0-A1 level, the biggest hurdle is spontaneous reaction. This generator helps you move beyond static textbook exercises by forcing you to process and answer questions in real-time.

The Power of "Wh-" Questions:

Learn to distinguish between Kdo (Who), Kde (Where), Kdy (When), and Kam (Where to). Recognizing the question word is 50% of the answer!

Case Recognition:

Many questions here use prepositions that trigger specific cases (e.g., S kým? - Instrumental, O čem? - Locative). Listen for the ending to know which form to use in your reply.

Optimization Strategy:

  • ✔ Step 1: Read the question out loud to practice your výslovnost (pronunciation).
  • ✔ Step 2: Try to answer with a full sentence, not just one word. (e.g., instead of "V Praze," say "Bydlím v Praze.")
