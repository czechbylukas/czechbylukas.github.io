Practice Dative (3rd Case)

Training Guide: Mastering the Dative (3. pád)

Active Recall: The Indirect Object & Recipients

The Dative case answers the question Komu? Čemu? (To whom? To what?). It is essential for communication verbs like "telling," "writing," and "calling."

Recipients and Gifts:

Whenever you give a gift (dárek) or write a message, the recipient must be in the Dative. Note the masculine endings: -ovi or -u.

Personal Expressions:

In Czech, you don't "have" an age; age is "to you." Practice Je mi 20 and Líbí se mi to to sound like a natural speaker.

Optimization Strategy:

  • ✔ Step 1: Learn the feminine shift from 'a' to 'e' (maminka → mamince) and 'e/i' endings.
  • ✔ Step 2: Use the preposition k / ke (towards) to practice movement toward people or places.
  • ✔ Step 3: Once you can recall the recipient forms, verify your knowledge in the 📝 Test.
