Training Guide: The Logic of Prefixes (Předpony)

Active Recall: Direction and Completion

In Czech, prefixes provide spatial direction or indicate that an action is finished. This practice session helps you visualize the "movement" behind the words.

Movement Logic:

Visualize the direction: od- is away, při- is towards, v- is inside, and vy- is outside. Mastering these makes understanding unknown verbs much easier.

Action Completion:

Prefixes like do- often mean finishing something (dodělat), while roz- often means starting or breaking apart (rozejít se).

Optimization Strategy:

  • ✔ Step 1: Identify the core verb first, then look at the prefix to determine the direction.
  • ✔ Step 2: Pay attention to reflexive particles like 'se' in sejít se (to meet).
  • ✔ Step 3: Once you can predict the meaning of the combined verb, proceed to the 📝 Test.
