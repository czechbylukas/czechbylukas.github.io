Training Guide: The -ovat and Irregular Group

Active Recall: -ovat to -uju Transformation

This session covers the -ovat class, where the suffix changes to -uju in the 1st person singular. This set also introduces essential irregular "movement" and "action" verbs like jít and psát.

The -ovat Rule:

It is a simple swap: pracovat becomes pracuju. Master this pattern to unlock hundreds of Czech verbs used in professional and academic settings.

Movement & Writing:

Irregulars like jdu (on foot), jedu (by vehicle), and píšu (to write) are included here because they are fundamental for describing your life.

Practice Optimization:

  • ✔ Step 1: Focus on the shift from -ovat to -uju for verbs like nakupovat.
  • ✔ Step 2: Pay attention to the location prepositions: v supermarketu vs z Prahy.
  • ✔ Step 3: Once you can recall the 1st-person forms for the whole list, go to the 📝 Test.
