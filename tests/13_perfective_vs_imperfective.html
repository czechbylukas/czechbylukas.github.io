Verification of Verbal Aspect

This assessment focuses on your ability to choose between an ongoing process and a completed action. This is the most nuanced part of Czech verb grammar.

Key Concepts:

The test includes pairs like pít/vypít, učit se/naučit se, and platit/zaplatit. You must select the verb that fits the completion status of the sentence.

Future Tense Logic:

Remember: Perfective verbs in the present form automatically indicate the future. This is a primary test criteria.

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Result-oriented: If the sentence implies the task is done, choose the perfective (prefixed) form.
  • ✔ Timer: 10 minutes. Aspect requires logic, so take a breath before clicking.
  • ✔ Prefix recognition: Pay attention to how uvařil (cooked) differs from vařil (was cooking).
