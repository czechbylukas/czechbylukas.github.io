Verification of 4th Case Usage

This assessment focuses on your ability to correctly identify the **direct object** in Czech sentences. You will be tested on noun endings for food, people, and objects after motion prepositions.

Key Concepts:

The test includes the usage of Dám si, prepositions of motion (na/za/před), and adjectives in the Accusative (mladý/jarní).

Survival Czech:

Being able to correctly conjugate nouns in the 4th case is the most important skill for dining and social interactions in the Czech Republic.

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Gender Matters: Remember that inanimate masculine nouns (like čaj) do not change in the 4th case.

  • ✔ Precision: Distinction between Petr miluje Evu and Eva miluje Petra is critical.
