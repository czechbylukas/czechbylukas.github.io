This assessment is the ultimate "Case Check." It requires you to know which pronoun form matches which preposition and verb structure.

The test includes prepositions like bez (Genitive), k (Dative), o (Locative), and s (Instrumental) paired with pronouns.

Question Words:

You will also be tested on the declension of kdo (who) and co (what), which are fundamental for asking questions in different cases.

Rules for Success:
