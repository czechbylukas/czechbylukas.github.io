Exam Instructions: Verb Prefix Rules

Verification of Prefix Knowledge

This assessment focuses on your ability to recognize and use Czech prefixes to modify the meaning of base verbs. This is a key skill for moving from A1 to A2 proficiency.

Key Concepts:

The test includes common prefixes (od-, do-, vy-, pře-) and their practical applications like podepsat (sign) and přejít (cross).

Directional Accuracy:

You will be asked to choose the correct prefix based on the desired movement or result of the action described in the sentence.

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Context: Read the whole sentence! The preposition later in the sentence often hints at the prefix (e.g., *z* Brna often uses *od-*).
  • ✔ Timer: 10 minutes. Thinking about spatial logic takes time, so stay focused!
  • ✔ Nuance: Understand the difference between přijít (arrive) and odejít (leave).
