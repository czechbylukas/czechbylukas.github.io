Exam Instructions: Official & Urgent Situations

Verification of A2 Administrative Competency

This assessment checks your ability to function within the Czech state system. It covers documents, fees, and the specific language used during emergencies like a nehoda or požár.

Key Concepts:

Be ready for terms like výpis z trestního rejstříku (criminal record) and úřední překlad (official translation).

Official Status:

Know the difference between povolení k zaměstnání (work permit) and povolení k pobytu (residence permit).

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Context: Understand when to use ztratit (to lose) vs. ukrást (to steal).
  • ✔ Timer: 15 minutes. Bureaucratic terms are long and specific—read each question carefully!
  • ✔ Verbs: Note the aspect pairs like ověřovat / ověřit.
