Exam Instructions: Daily Life Mastery

Verification of A2 Routine Vocabulary

This assessment focuses on your ability to describe "a day in the life." You must choose the correct verb aspect based on whether the action is a habit or a completed task.

Key Concepts:

Expect questions about hygiene (pastu, kartáček, ručník) and household management (prací prášek, žehlení).

Movement Verbs:

Remember the difference between going on foot (chodit/jít) and by vehicle (jezdit/jet).

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Context Matters: Words like "obvykle" (usually) require the first verb in the pair; "dnes" (today) often requires the second.
  • ✔ Timer: 15 minutes. Precision with se/si is crucial here.
  • ✔ Nouns: Be careful with the spelling of mýdlo and ručník.
