Exam Instructions: Survival in Transit

Verification of A2 Travel Competency

This assessment simulates real-world travel scenarios. You will be tested on your ability to understand schedules, buy tickets, and follow street directions.

Key Concepts:

Be ready for questions using Jak dlouho... (How long...) and the distinction between přijet (arrive) and odjet (depart).

Prepositions:

Master the use of naproti (across from) and okolo (about/around) for distance and location.

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Politeness: Many phrases start with Prosím or Chtěl bych—pay attention to the formal tone.
  • ✔ Timer: 15 minutes. Travel involves many full sentences, so read carefully!
  • ✔ Logic: Ensure you know the difference between vlevo (on the left) and doleva (to the left).
