Exam Instructions: Environmental Literacy

Verification of A2 Natural Vocabulary

This assessment checks your ability to describe your surroundings. It covers everything from hvězdy in the night sky to reporting a bouřka (storm) or mráz (frost).

Key Concepts:

Be ready for verbs of natural action: foukat (to blow), mrznout (to freeze), and svítit (to shine).

Flora and Fauna:

Differentiate between strom (tree), květina (flower), and rostlina (plant).

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Weather States: Note that words like jasno and teplo often describe the current state of the environment.
  • ✔ Timer: 15 minutes. Watch out for similar-sounding words like mrak (cloud) and mráz (frost).
  • ✔ Nature: Know your řeka (river) from your jezero (lake).
