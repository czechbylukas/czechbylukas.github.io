Exam Instructions: Classroom Competency

Verification of A2 Academic Vocabulary

This assessment checks your ability to navigate the school environment. It covers everything from administrative staff like the školník to the types of exams you might take (písemný vs. ústní).

Key Concepts:

Be ready for verbs like vysvětlit (explain), opakovat (repeat/review), and zaškrtnout (cross/check an answer).

Education Levels:

Differentiate between základní, střední, and vysokoškolské education.

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Roles: Distinguish between a žák (pupil) and a student.
  • ✔ Timer: 15 minutes. Note that in Czech, a 'grade' is a známka.
  • ✔ Verbs: Remember to use the correct case after verbs like učit se (+ Accusative).
