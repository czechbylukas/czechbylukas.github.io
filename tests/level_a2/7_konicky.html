Exam Instructions: Social Conversation

Verification of A2 Leisure Vocabulary

This assessment evaluates your ability to engage in small talk and describe your personal life. You will be tested on hobby-related nouns, verbs of motion, and expressions of preference.

Key Concepts:

Be prepared for mít volno (to be off work) and activities like sbírat (to collect) or chovat (to breed/keep pets).

Time Off:

Distinguish between víkend (weekend) and svátek (public holiday or name day).

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Prepositions: Note the difference between hrát na... and just hrát....
  • ✔ Timer: 15 minutes. Focus on the relationship between nouns and their corresponding verbs.
  • ✔ Activity: Know the difference between a procházka (walk) and a výlet (trip).
