Exam Instructions: Professional Competency

Verification of A2 Career Vocabulary

This assessment checks your readiness for the Czech labor market. You will be tested on your ability to identify roles, describe working conditions, and understand salary components.

Key Concepts:

Be prepared for terms like úřad práce (job centre), pracovní doba (working hours), and praxe (experience).

Work Environments:

Match the profession to the location: úředník in an úřad, or dělník in a továrna.

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Accuracy: Differentiate between plat (salary) and mzda (wage).
  • ✔ Timer: 15 minutes. Professional terms require careful reading!
  • ✔ Situations: Know terms for nezaměstnaný (unemployed) and mateřská dovolená (maternity leave).
