Exam Instructions: Medical Situations

Verification of A2 Healthcare Competency

This assessment checks your ability to communicate in a medical context. You will be tested on anatomy, common illnesses like chřipka, and the terminology for zdravotní pojištění.

Key Concepts:

Be prepared for terms like vysoký tlak, očkování (vaccination), and recept (prescription).

Emergency & Staff:

Identify roles: lékař, zdravotní sestra, and ošetřovatel. Know when to visit pohotovost (emergency).

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Grammar: Remember the construction bolí mě... is used for singular parts, bolí mě oči for plural.
  • ✔ Timer: 15 minutes. Medical terms often have specific endings (e.g., -a for rýma or dieta).
  • ✔ Action: Know the difference between zranit se and léčit se.
