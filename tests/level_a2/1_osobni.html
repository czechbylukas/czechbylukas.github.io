Exam Instructions: Navigating Personal Information

Verification of A2 Descriptive Vocabulary

This assessment covers the terminology required for official registration and social integration. It is designed to simulate real-world scenarios like job interviews and administrative appointments.

Key Concepts:

Expect questions on marital status (svobodný, ženatý, vdaná) and the correct use of verbs like narodit se and stěhovat se.

Personality & Appearance:

You must distinguish between internal traits (zodpovědný, líný) and external features (kudrnatý, plnoštíhlý).

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Gender Precision: For "to get married," remember: oženit se (for men) and vdát se (for women).
  • ✔ Timer: 15 minutes. This is a larger vocabulary set, so pace yourself!
  • ✔ Context: Read the prompts carefully to see if they ask for a first name or a full surname.
