Exam Instructions: Practical Transactions

Verification of A2 Shopping Vocabulary

This assessment evaluates your ability to function in commercial environments. It covers everything from selecting a velikost (size) to understanding a směnný kurz (exchange rate).

Key Concepts:

Expect scenarios involving the pošta (sending a balík), the čistírna (dry cleaners), and the lékárna.

Verbal Aspect:

Pay attention to verbs like vybírat/vybrat (to withdraw money) and platit/zaplatit (to pay).

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Precision: Note the difference between obchod (store) and obchodní centrum (mall).
  • ✔ Timer: 15 minutes. There are many specialized terms here!
  • ✔ State of items: Know your otevřeno vs. zavřeno.
