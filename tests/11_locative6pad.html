Exam Instructions: Locative Declension Rules

Verification of 6th Case Usage

This assessment focuses on your ability to use the Locative case for locations and topics. It is a critical test for navigating the Czech Republic and describing your environment.

Key Concepts:

The test includes usage of v Brně, na pláži, and describing what is in a byt or pokoj.

Optional Endings:

Be prepared for nouns that allow multiple endings, such as byt/bytu or soused/sousedovi. Both variations are tested.

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Static vs. Motion: Remember that v and na in this test refer to being there, not going there.
  • ✔ Timer: 10 minutes. Accuracy with softening (e.g., r → ř in moře) is key.
  • ✔ People: Ensure you recognize the -ovi ending for animate masculines.
