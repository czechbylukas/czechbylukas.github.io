Exam Instructions: Advanced Verb Stems

Verification of Aspectual Stem Changes

This assessment focuses on your ability to distinguish between verbs that change their internal structure to express aspect. This is essential for talking about future plans.

Key Concepts:

The test covers pairs like dostát/dostávat and připravit/připravovat. You must identify which form describes the ongoing habit.

Practical Tasks:

Expect questions about chores (uklízet, luxovat) and commerce (koupit, prodat), as these are common in A2 level exams.

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Vowel check: Watch for small changes like ztratit vs ztrácet.
  • ✔ Timer: 10 minutes. Accuracy is key when stems look similar!
  • ✔ Logic: Remember that "to be doing" is always the longer, more complex stem in these pairs.
