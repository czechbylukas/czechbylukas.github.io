Exam Instructions: Modifiers and Degrees

Verification of Adjective and Adverb Mastery

This assessment evaluates your ability to describe qualities, compare objects, and modify verbs with adverbs. It includes character traits, physical descriptions, and irregular forms.

Key Concepts:

The test includes three degrees of comparison (krásný, krásnější, nejkrásnější) and the distinction between adjectives and adverbs (rychlý vs. rychle).

Vocabulary Range:

Expect questions on personality (upřímný, tvrdohlavý) and environment (moderní, špinavý).

Rules for Success:

  • ✔ Adverb Check: If describing an action, look for the -e/-ě ending (e.g., mluvit česky).
  • ✔ Timer: 10 minutes. Superlatives can be long words, so type carefully!
  • ✔ Precision: Be careful with the 'h' to 'ž' shift in dlouhý → delší.
