Exam Instructions: Strengthening Your Conjugation

The Four Major Verb Groups

This middle-level assessment ensures you can accurately apply the endings for the most common verb classes. It’s the bridge between simple repetition and full fluency.

Verb Types Included:

  • Dělám Group (-at): The most straightforward regular pattern.
  • Rozumím Group (-ět/-it): Verbs ending in long -ím.
  • Piju/Studuju Group (-ovat): Changing the infinitive to the -uje- stem.

Common Pitfalls:

Watch out for the "Oni" (They) forms—these are often the most difficult to remember correctly in the -e and -í groups.

Study Tips:

  • ✔ Check the Stem: Look at the infinitive before you conjugate. Does it end in -ovat? Switch to -uj-.
  • ✔ Pronouns: The ending tells you who is doing the action. You don't always need já, ty, on..., but the verb must match!
  • ✔ Timer: 10 minutes. This level tests your speed in recognizing which group a verb belongs to.
