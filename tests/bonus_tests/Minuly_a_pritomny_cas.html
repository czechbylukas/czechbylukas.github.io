Exam Instructions: Navigating Time in Czech

Switching Between Tenses

This assessment tests your ability to identify time markers and apply the correct grammatical rules for both the Present Tense (conjugation) and the Past Tense (l-participle + auxiliary verbs).

Present Tense Logic:

  • Endings: Match the verb ending to the person (e.g., -ám, -íš, -uje).
  • Time Markers: Look for words like teď, dnes, obvykle, v tuhle chvíli.

Past Tense Logic:

  • Auxiliary Verbs: Don't forget jsem, jsi, jsme, jste for 1st and 2nd person.
  • Gender Agreement: The verb ending changes for Masculine (-l), Feminine (-la), and Neuter (-lo).
  • Time Markers: Look for včera, minulý týden, před hodinou.

Top Strategies:

  • ✔ Scan for Adverbs: Before you conjugate, find the "Time Anchor" in the sentence to decide the tense.
  • ✔ Check the Subject: In the past tense, you must know the gender of the person speaking or being spoken about.
  • ✔ Timer: 10 minutes. Mixed tests require more focus, so use your time to verify that you haven't skipped an auxiliary verb in the past tense forms.
