The Two Ways to Look Forward

In this middle-level test, we move beyond just conjugating the verb být. You must decide between the standard compound future and the prefix future used for verbs of motion.

Standard Compound Future:

  • Formula: Budu, budeš, bude... + Infinitive.
  • Usage: Most standard A1 verbs (e.g., budu dělat, budeme spát).

Motion Verb Exceptions:

Remember: Verbs of motion never use "budu". Instead, they use a prefix: Pů- (půjdu) or Po- (poletím, pojedu).

Success Tips:

  • ✔ Identify the Verb: Is it a motion verb (jít, jet, letět)? If yes, stay away from the word "budu"!
  • ✔ Word Order: In questions and complex sentences, keep the infinitive and the auxiliary verb close together.
  • ✔ Timer: 10 minutes. Use your time to double-check that you haven't used a present-tense ending where an infinitive belongs.
