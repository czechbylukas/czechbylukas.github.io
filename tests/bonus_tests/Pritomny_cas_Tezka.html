Exam Instructions: Advanced A1 Conjugation

Beyond the Standard Endings

This "Hard Level" assessment targets the verbs that beginners usually struggle with. It moves past the simple -á and -í patterns into irregular stems and consonant changes.

What is tested:

  • Irregular Verbs: Být, chtít, jíst, vědět.
  • -ovat Verbs: Changes to -uje- (e.g., pracovat -> pracuje).
  • Stem Changes: Verbs like psát (píše) or brát (bere).

Modal Verbs:

Be careful with moct (můžu/můžeš) and smět. These are essential for daily A1 communication but tricky to spell.

Success Tips:

  • ✔ Identify the Group: Even irregular verbs often follow the -u, -eš, -e or -ím, -íš, -í ending patterns once you find the stem.
  • ✔ Negative Forms: Remember that "to not have" is nemít, but "to not be" is není (except for 1st person nejsem).
  • ✔ Timer: 10 minutes. This test is designed to see if you can conjugate under pressure without overthinking the irregulars.
