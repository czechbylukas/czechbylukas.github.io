Exam Instructions: Your First Verbs

The "Dělám" Conjugation Group

This entry-level assessment focuses on regular verbs ending in -at. This is the most stable verb group in the Czech language and forms the backbone of basic conversation.

Common Verbs Included:

  • Dělat: To do/make.
  • Mít: To have.
  • Hledat: To look for.
  • Čekat: To wait.

The Goal:

Focus on matching the subject (Já, Ty, On/Ona...) to the correct ending (-ám, -áš, -á...).

Study Tips:

  • ✔ Consistency: In this group, the long -á- stays in almost every form.
  • ✔ They (Oni): Remember that for this group, the "They" form ends in -ají.
  • ✔ Timer: 10 minutes. Don't rush! This is about accuracy and getting the long vowels right.

Technical Metadata: Dataset grammar_pres_tense_easy_a1. Focuses exclusively on the first conjugation class (regular -at verbs).
