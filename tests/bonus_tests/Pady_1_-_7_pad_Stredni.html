Exam Instructions: Expanding Your Declension Skills

Building a Strong Foundation

This middle-level test bridges the gap between basic identification and advanced mastery. You will encounter all 7 cases, but the sentences are designed with clear triggers—mostly common prepositions—to help you identify the correct pád.

Key Areas of Focus:

  • Standard Prepositions: S (with), k (to), do (into), and bez (without).
  • High-Frequency Verbs: Verbs like dát (to give), pomoct (to help), and mluvit o (to talk about).
  • Gender Basics: Recognizing the standard endings for Feminine, Neuter, and Masculine nouns.

Why take this test?

It helps you internalize the most common patterns so that using the Instrumental or Dative becomes second nature in daily conversation.

Exam Tips:

  • ✔ Keywords: Look for "prepositional anchors" like pro, na, v, or u. They are your best friends in this level.
  • ✔ Singular Focus: This level focuses primarily on singular forms to ensure your core declension is solid before moving to plurals.
  • ✔ Timer: 10 minutes. A balanced pace is better than rushing—focus on selecting the correct gender ending.
