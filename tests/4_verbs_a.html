This test evaluates your grasp of the 1st conjugation class. You must demonstrate correct use of the first-person singular forms and the ability to place verbs correctly within a Czech sentence.

The test includes verbs like dát si (ordering food), koukat se (reflexive verbs), and mít (to have). Sentence translation and form selection are key components.

Achieving 80% or higher indicates readiness for the second and third conjugation groups (-it, -et, -ovat).
