Study Guide: The Czech Gastronomy Reference

Eating and Drinking in Czechia

This vocabulary list is essential for anyone wanting to enjoy the rich culinary landscape of the Czech Republic. It covers everything from formal menu items to colloquial terms like dýško.

Traditional Dishes:

Master complex names like svíčková na smetaně and plněná paprika. These are staples in every Czech household and restaurant.

Logistics:

Beyond just food, learn the roles: kuchař, číšník, servírka and the components of a meal: snídaně, oběd, večeře.

The HáCZech Learning Path:

  • ✔ Step 1: Learn the core verbs first: pít, objednat, zaplatit.
  • ✔ Step 2: Focus on categories (meat types, side dishes, drinks).
