Shopping, Services, and Errands in Czechia

This vocabulary list provides the specific terminology needed to navigate the Czech economy. It is divided into retail, clothing, health, and administrative services.

Clothing & Fitting:

Beyond basic items like tričko or kalhoty, learn the actions: zkusit si (try on) and vyměnit (exchange).

Banking & Mail:

Errands require specific verbs: vyplnit (fill out a form), poslat (send a letter), and vybrat peníze (withdraw money).

  • ✔ Step 2: Master the payment vocabulary—it's used in every single interaction.
