Study Guide: The Environmental Directory

The World Around You in Czech

This vocabulary list is a vital resource for hikers, pet owners, and anyone living in the Czech Republic. It covers the beauty of příroda and the practical reality of daily počasí.

Landscape Elements:

Master the geographical features: hora, les, pole, and jezero. Learn the astronomical basics like měsíc and hvězda.

Weather Phenomena:

Understand the conditions: from a peaceful jasno to a dangerous bouřka. Learn to talk about teplota and vlhkost.

The HáCZech Learning Path:

  • ✔ Step 1: Start with basic nouns (sun, rain, snow).
  • ✔ Step 2: Focus on the adjectives and adverbs describing the current weather (warm, cold, cloudy).
  • ✔ Step 3: Finalize this module with the 🧠 Practice and 📝 Test links.
