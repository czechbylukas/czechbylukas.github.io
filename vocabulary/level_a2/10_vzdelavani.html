Study Guide: The Academic Directory

Learning in the Czech Republic

This vocabulary list is a fundamental resource for anyone attending a kurz, school, or university in Czechia. It covers the physical environment of the school and the intellectual actions required for success.

Subjects & Fields:

Master the names of fields like právo, medicína, and informatika. Learn how to describe your obor (field of study).

The Classroom Environment:

Identify locations like the tělocvična, jídelna, and šatna. Know who is who: from the ředitel to the uklízečka.

The HáCZech Learning Path:

  • ✔ Step 1: Focus on the verbs of action (write, read, count, explain).
  • ✔ Step 2: Learn the terminology for testing and grades (známka, chyba, test).
  • ✔ Step 3: Finalize this module with the 🧠 Practice and 📝 Test links.
