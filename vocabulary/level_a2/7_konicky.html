Study Guide: The Leisure Directory

Enjoying Life in the Czech Republic

This vocabulary list focuses on how to express what makes you happy outside of work or school. It includes common pastimes like zahradničení and social visits.

Games & Instruments:

Learn nouns like šachy, karty, kulečník and instruments like housle. Use the correct prepositional phrases to describe playing them.

Active Leisure:

Actions include plavat (swim), tancovat (dance), and fotografovat (take photos). Don't forget sbírat—a very common hobby in Czech forests!
