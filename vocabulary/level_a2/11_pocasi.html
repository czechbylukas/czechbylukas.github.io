Study Guide: The Medical Directory

Body, Health, and Care in Czechia

This vocabulary list provides the terms necessary for discussing your physical state and navigating the medical world. It ranges from basic anatomie to formal administrative terms like předepsat lék.

Symptoms and Ailments:

Master the vocabulary for the most common issues: průjem, zácpa, kašel, and alergie. Be ready to describe a rána (wound) or úraz.

Special Conditions:

Includes terms for těhotenství (pregnancy), zdravotně postižené (disabled persons), and the process of očkování.

The HáCZech Learning Path:

  • ✔ Step 1: Learn the body parts using visual aids.
  • ✔ Step 2: Focus on the verbs used with the doctor (objednat se, předepsat, vyšetřit).
