Study Guide: The "Short vs. Long" Verb System

Czech Stem Changes in Aspect Pairs

While many verbs use prefixes to change aspect, these high-frequency verbs use stem alternations. This vocabulary list is your roadmap to understanding how verbs "expand" to show process.

Common Patterns:

Look for the -vat suffix (prodávat, dostávat) or the shift from -it to -at/-et (uklidit → uklízet, ztratit → ztrácet).

Future Tense Context:

Mastering these pairs is the first step to future tense. You must know if a verb is Imperfective to know whether to use "budu" or just the conjugated present form.
