Essential Czech Question Words List

To communicate effectively, you must be able to ask. This Czech vocabulary set covers 30+ question words ranging from simple adverbs to complex prepositional pronouns like bez koho and pro co.

Time & Frequency:

Learn to ask about time using V kolik (At what time) and frequency with Jak často. These are critical for making plans and appointments.

Quantity & Selection:

Differentiate between Kolik (How much/many), Jaký (What kind of), and Který (Which). Understanding these helps in shopping and choosing items.
