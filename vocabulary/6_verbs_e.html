Czech -ovat and Essential Irregular Verbs

The -ovat group (the -uju class) is extremely common in modern Czech. This list also includes several irregular verbs that don't fit the standard patterns but are used in almost every daily conversation.

Work, Study, & Travel:

These verbs allow you to talk about your life's structure. Master pracovat, studovat, and cestovat to answer basic questions about yourself.

The Irregular Core:

Verbs like jít/jedu and číst/psát have unique stem changes. We grouped them here because they are fundamental for reaching beginner A1 fluency.
